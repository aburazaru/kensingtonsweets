About Kensington Sweets

est 2013

Welcome to Kensington Sweets. We create a variety of cakeballs and cupcakes for any occasion. Sweeten up your next party with some of these delicious treats. We have a wide variety of flavor/coating combinations to make sure you have something for everyone there! Contact us today to place an order!

FLAVORS & COATINGS!

We have several filling flavor options available as well as different coating flavors and designs.

Filling Flavors

Chocolate, Yellow Cake, Red Velvet, Oreo, Peanut Butter, Strawberry, Carrot Cake, Funfetty and don't forget to ask about seasonal flavors!

Coatings

Milk Chocolate and White Chocolate available in plain (smooth) coatings or a drizzle pattern. We can also doll them up with sprinkles or a light cookie crumble.

Minimum Orders and Pricing

Any flavor combination listed above will be $1.00 per ball with a minumum order of 24 for each flavor/coating combination desired.
